Jobsiee

A smarter way to find and apply for jobs. Availabile for Windows and macOS.

JobSieve is a desktop app that lets you manage the entire job application process in one focused workflow.

Jobsieve automatically scans hundreds of job listings across major platforms such as LinkedIn, Glassdoor and Indeed. It filters listings by role, location, language and job type and removes irrelevant matches early so you can focus only on suitable opportunities. JobSieve compares filtered jobs with your CV and ranks them by relevance. It highlights strengths, gaps, required skills and role alignment, helping you prioritize the applications most worth your time. JobSieve rewrites your CVs and cover letters for selected roles using your own documents as the source. It keeps your experience truthful while adapting the wording to each job listing, role and application context.

Visit the Jobsieve website for more information.
